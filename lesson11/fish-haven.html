Preston is a city located in Franklin County in the US state of Idaho. In the 2010 Census it had a population of 5,204 inhabitants and a population density of 301.78 people per km².

Preston is located at coordinates 42 ° 5′56 ″ N 111 ° 52′42 ″ W. According to the United States Census Bureau, Preston has a total area of 17.24 km², of which 17.22 km² corresponds to the mainland and (0.12%) 0.02 km² is water.

According to the 2010 census, there were 5204 people residing in Preston. The population density was 301.78 inhabitants / km². Of the 5204 inhabitants, Preston was made up of 0.09% white, 0.21% were African American, 0.73% were Native American, 0.21% were Asian, 0.08% were Pacific Islanders, 3.32% were from other races and 1.75 % belonged to two or more races. Of the total population 7.51% were Hispanic or Latino of any race.
